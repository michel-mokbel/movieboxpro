Privacy Policy for MovieBox Pro

Effective Date: January 22, 2025

At MovieBox Pro, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we process, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your data. By using the MovieBox Pro application, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, or payment details as no login, sign-up, or transactions are required.
  • No User Accounts: The app does not store user credentials or profile information.

2. Information We May Collect

To provide functionality and enhance app performance, we may collect:

  • Device Information: Non-identifiable information such as device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data to understand app usage and improve features (e.g., app crashes, session duration).

3. How We Use Collected Data

The data we collect is used for:

  • Improving app performance and compatibility.
  • Troubleshooting technical issues and enhancing the user experience.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

MovieBox Pro integrates with third-party services, including:

  • OMDb API: Used to fetch movie details (e.g., titles, ratings, posters). No data is stored permanently. Read their Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube: Redirects users to YouTube for trailer searches. YouTube may process your data per their Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics Tools: Aggregated data (e.g., Firebase Analytics) may be used to measure app performance, without collecting personal information.

5. Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected, we do not retain user-specific data. Any analytics data is anonymized and stored according to third-party service policies.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices to secure the app and prevent unauthorized access. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure. Users are encouraged to take precautions when using online services.

7. Children's Privacy

MovieBox Pro is not intended for users under 13 years of age. If we become aware that data from a child under 13 has been collected, it will be promptly deleted.

8. User Rights

As no personal data is collected, users do not need to request access, deletion, or corrections. For inquiries, contact us.
